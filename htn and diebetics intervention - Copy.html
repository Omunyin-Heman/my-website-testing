Interventions for Hypertension and Diabetes

Interventions for Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

  1. Dietary Changes
  2. Regular Physical Activity
  3. Weight Management
  4. Medication Adherence
  5. Stress Management

Interventions for Diabetes

  1. Blood Glucose Control
  2. Nutritional Counseling
  3. Physical Activity
  4. Foot Care and Monitoring
  5. Medication Management
  6. Diabetes Education and Self-Management
  7. Psychosocial Support

Integrated Approach

By integrating interventions for hypertension and diabetes, healthcare providers can enhance patient care through a multidisciplinary team approach, continuous monitoring and evaluation, and personalized patient-centered care.
