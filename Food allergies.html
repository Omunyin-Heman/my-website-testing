Food Allergies Management at Hemans Nutrition and Wellness Clinic

1. Understanding Food Allergies

Education on food allergies and their impact on nutrition. Includes:

  • Explanation of food allergies vs. food intolerances.
  • Common food allergens and their symptoms.
  • Importance of avoiding allergens and reading food labels.

2. Nutritional Strategies for Food Allergies

Guidelines for managing food allergies through diet. Topics covered:

  • Substituting allergenic foods with safe alternatives.
  • Ensuring balanced nutrition while avoiding allergens.
  • Meal planning and preparation tips for allergen-free diets.

3. Reading Food Labels

Skills for identifying allergens on food labels. Includes:

  • Understanding ingredient lists and allergen warnings.
  • Hidden sources of allergens and cross-contamination risks.
  • Tools and resources for interpreting food labels accurately.

4. Meal Planning for Food Allergies

Practical tips and resources for allergen-free meal planning. Includes:

  • Weekly meal planning templates for allergen-free diets.
  • Recipes and meal ideas tailored for specific allergens.
  • Cooking techniques to prevent cross-contamination.

5. Educational Resources and Support

Connecting patients with resources for managing food allergies. Includes:

  • Tools for tracking allergen exposure and symptoms.
  • Access to support groups and counseling services.
  • Additional reading materials and online resources.

Empowering patients with knowledge and skills for managing food allergies nutritionally.
