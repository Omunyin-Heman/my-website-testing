Diabetic Management and Cancer Therapies at Hemans Nutrition and Wellness Clinic

1. Understanding Diabetes and Nutrition

Education on diabetes types and their impact on nutrition. Includes:

  • Overview of Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes.
  • Glycemic index/load and its role in meal planning.
  • Importance of balanced nutrition for managing blood sugar levels.

2. Nutritional Strategies for Diabetic Patients

Guidelines for managing diabetes through diet. Topics covered:

  • Carbohydrate counting and meal timing.
  • Fiber intake recommendations.
  • Role of protein and healthy fats.

3. Specific Nutritional Needs for Diabetic Cancer Patients

Customizing nutrition plans for diabetic patients undergoing cancer treatment. Includes:

  • Managing dietary adjustments for cancer symptoms and diabetes control.
  • Nutritional support strategies during chemotherapy or radiation.

4. Medications and Nutrient Interactions

Understanding diabetes medications and their interactions with cancer treatments. Includes:

  • Impact of insulin and oral medications on diet and blood glucose levels.
  • Managing medication schedules and potential side effects.

5. Complementary Therapies

Exploring complementary therapies to support diabetes and cancer care. Topics include:

  • Benefits of acupuncture, yoga, and other therapies.
  • Integrating complementary therapies with nutrition strategies.

6. Supporting Diabetes Management During Cancer Treatment

Strategies for monitoring and managing blood glucose levels during cancer treatment. Includes:

  • Adjusting insulin or medications based on treatment schedules.
  • Dietary tips for maintaining stable blood sugar levels.

7. Meal Planning and Preparation for Diabetic Patients

Practical tips and resources for diabetic-friendly meal planning. Includes:

  • Portion control and balanced plate method.
  • Healthy cooking techniques and recipe ideas.

8. Educational Resources and Support

Connecting patients with resources for diabetes self-management and support. Includes:

  • Tools for monitoring blood glucose levels and medications.
  • Access to support groups and counseling services.
